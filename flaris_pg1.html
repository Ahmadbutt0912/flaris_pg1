Flaris LAR 01 defines a new category of personal jet, being the only one able to take off from small grass airstrips . It is easy to fly and offers a much more affordable price and lower maintenance costs than other business aircraft.

›SPECTACULARLY LOW VARIABLE COSTS ‹
